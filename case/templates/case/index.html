Better Business Cases tool

Here is a super brief overview of what better business cases are about.

There are five dimensions, you add a range of options for each dimension, and then score it on some criteria. The path with the highest score is the one to go for.

Here is some information on how this tool works.

This tool will take you through each of the five dimensions of a business case: Scale, Scope & Location; Delivery; 3; 4; 5.

For each dimension, you will complete four programme options: do nothing (baseline); minimum work; intermediate work; ambitious work.

You will then score each scenario against the five criteria of good business cases.

The output will be the best business case.
